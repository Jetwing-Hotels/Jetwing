## Project Overview

Development at Jetwing Symphony cannot be separated from sustainable tourism. The six-pillar approach for sustainability stresses climate action, resource efficiency, heritage preservation, community involvement, workforce development, and impact sourcing.

This project aims to develop a **Sustainability Management Platform**. The proposed system will capture and analyze information on the environmental and social performance of Jetwing Symphony properties. This will be achieved by integrating with energy meters, wastewater treatment facilities, and other existing systems to develop real-time dashboards as well as certification-ready reports.
